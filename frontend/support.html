Frequently asked questions

How can I book a room?

Go to the Rooms page, choose your dates and number of guests, then select a suitable room.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your reservation from the My bookings page.

How do I choose the right room?

You can filter rooms by capacity. The system will show rooms that match the selected number of guests.

Do I need an account?

For the final version, users will need an account to manage bookings and view reservation history.
